We Get It

We understand your frustration with the existing alternative and augmentative communication (AAC) products.
We get it. This is why SpeakYourMind Foundation exists.
With your help, we intend on making the days of outrageously expensive, unreliable, antiquated, and difficult to use AAC devices a thing of the past.

Who We Help

Our goal is to help those who have slipped through the cracks because the devices available to them do not meet their needs or budget.
Very often, people with the following disabilities tend to reach out to us the most:

  • Traumatic brain injury (TBI)
  • Stroke
  • Spinal cord injury
  • ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease)
  • Cerebral Palsy (CP)
  • Rhett Syndrome
  • Muscular dystrophy
  • Guillain-Barré
  • Progressive supranuclear palsy (PSP)
  • Spinal muscular atrophy (SMA)

But... we're here for absolutely anyone who needs us.
